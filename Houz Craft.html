About Us

To design, is to dream. To implement, is to realise that dream. We at Houz Craft listen to the story behind that dream as well as help you realise it.
Founded in 2013, we have been into the field of interior design and architecture for several years and have created numerous beautiful homes and commercial spaces. Let it be a complex, design oriented space or a functional, budget oriented space, we make sure that the project is delivered within the promised timeline and budget without compromising on the quality. We believe in transparency, punctuality and providing an anxiety free experience.
Our services: Modular work, carpentry, furniture, false ceiling, lighting, painting, electrical work, plumbing work, civil work, 3d design, 2d design, architectural services, curtains, wallpapers, tiles, etc

Our Process

Pay 5% of the estimated cost.Our team measures and analyses your house. We prepare a 3d design based on your requirements. Finalise design as per your taste.

Pay 45% of the final cost. Our team starts procuring material for production. Work will begin at the factory as well as on site for civil, false ceiling, electrical, plumbing, etc if any.

Pay 50% of the final cost on delivery of modular material to the site. We proceed with procurement of hardware and installation.

We conduct a thorough inspection to ensure everything is up to the mark. Finally, we handover your furnished home along with a warranty certificate.

We provide free yearly service which includes free replacement of defective products under warranty.
